Monumental Venues

Courtyard of Villafranca Castle

The Castle-Palace of the Marquises of Villafranca is not a mere monumental location or a rented space; it represents an incalculable legacy. Transmitted in an uninterrupted family heritage since the 15th century, it was the residence of the brilliant pianist Marita Caro, mother of Pedro Halffter, and currently constitutes the private residence of the director and composer himself. By crossing its doors, the spectator enters the intimacy of our home, transforming the fortress into an active emotional participant.

Under a captivating and dim twilight illumination, the courtyard hosts nocturnal concerts at 21:30 h. The attendees, far from being mere spectators, become privileged guests in the family bosom, eliminating the traditional distance between the stage and the audience. With a capacity strictly limited to 300 seats, the seats will be occupied strictly on a first-come, first-served basis, allowing the acoustics and the weight of centuries of family heritage to merge into an unrepeatable musical experience.

📍 Calle del Castillo, s/n, 24500 Villafranca del Bierzo (León)

Church of San Nicolás

The Church of San Nicolás de Bari is a monumental 17th-century complex that constitutes one of the most imposing examples of Herrerian-influenced Baroque architecture in El Bierzo. Historically linked to the former Jesuit College, this emblematic temple stands out for its sober facade and its imposing presence in the heart of the town's historic center, opening its doors to offer a corner of enormous heritage wealth that invites contemplation and the enjoyment of the arts.

For this space, the concerts will have an evening character at 20:30 h and will take place taking advantage of the magnificent natural acoustic conditions of its sacred architecture, with a capacity strictly limited to 200 seats. As the seats are not numbered, they will be occupied strictly on a first-come, first-served basis on the day of the event, ensuring that the solemnity of the temple and the musical experience merge in an unforgettable way.

📍 Plaza de San Nicolás, s/n, 24500 Villafranca del Bierzo (León)
