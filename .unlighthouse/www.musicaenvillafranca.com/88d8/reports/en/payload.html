Our Essence

WHERE MUSIC TRANSFORMS INTO PILGRIMAGE.

Inspired by the magic of the great European summer festivals, Música en Villafranca was born to create a perfect symbiosis between the town's rich monumental heritage and international artistic excellence. A gathering where the stones of the Castle and the sacred acoustics of San Nicolás come alive through the greatest works of history.

Here, we completely eliminate the traditional barriers between the stage and the audience. Through a circular stage setup and absolute complicity, we invite each spectator to shed preconceived ideas, forget the noise of the world, and transform into a true musical pilgrim.

Venues with History

More than monumental locations, these are true ancestral homes. By taking a seat in the Castle's Courtyard—an uninterrupted family heritage of the pianist Marita Caro since the 15th century and current private residence of director Pedro Halffter—you enter the intimacy of our own home, erasing any barrier between stage and audience.

Courtyard of Villafranca Castle

An exclusive invitation to the ancestral home of the Caro family. Nocturnal concerts under dim lighting in the director's private residence.

Church of San Nicolás

The 17th-century Jesuit heart. Wood and stone enveloping chamber music.
